## What To Ask Them Afterwards

1. What did you think HeyMies was for after two minutes?
2. Where did you hesitate?
3. Did Mia's wording feel helpful or too automated?
4. Did any email not arrive?
5. Would you trust the lead handover enough to act on it?
6. What one thing would make this feel more real?
